Credit Card Fraud Detection

For our project we decided to tackle the issue of credit card fraud. First off, what is credit card fraud?
Credit card fraud is when someone who isn't authorized to use your card makes purchases that you didn't authorize.
How big of a problem is this? Researches suggest that 86 percent of consumers were a victim of credit card fraud in 2020.
For this issue we wanted to create a model that would assist in indicating if a specific transaction was fraudulent. We took data from a well prepared dataset on Kaggle which we used to train out data. After this we balanced our dataset so we can train our model optimally. The model we used was able to predict based off of the balanced dataset we had trained on if a transaction was fradulent or not.
